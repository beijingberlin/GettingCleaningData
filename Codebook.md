# Variables

subject: integer between 1 and 30, identifying a person in the study

activity: one of six activities (WALKING, WALKING\_UPSTAIRS, WALKING\_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING)

the remaining variables are recordings of smartphone sensors. Each of
the variables are averages over repeated measurements for each subject
and activity.
